Privacy Policy


RedTurn Software and Buzzrd respects your privacy. We are committed to protecting all personal information collected from you. To illustrate this commitment, we provide this Privacy Policy ("Policy"). Within the Policy we explain how we collect, store, and use the information you provide.

1. Introduction
This Policy covers the Buzzrd application and the data collected through it us by you.

We may change this Privacy Policy to better protect your privacy. When changes are made we will notify you based on you Buzzrd User Settings. Notification may be done directly through the Buzzrd application. You should review the Policy before connecting to Buzzrd.

2. Personal Information
i. What we collect
User Name
Password
(Optional) First Name
(Optional) Last Name
(Optional) Gender
Location of user when contributions are made to Buzzrd. (Based on Location Services of your mobile device)

ii. How we use it
Buzzrd only users your information to validate your access to the application. Buzzrd in no way will sell this information to any third party.

iii. How we store it
All data collected is stored in a secure manner. Passwords and Usernames are stored in a one way hash, which prevents anyone from viewing this data even in the unlikely event of a data breach.

3. Information Collected Automatically
We collect time, date, and location of all contributions to Buzzrd. Each contribution made to Buzzrd is associated with your username. This information will not be used for anything other than displaying data to you and other users in Buzzrd.

4. CONTACT US
If you have any questions, complaints, or comments regarding this Policy, please contact us by the means listed below.
